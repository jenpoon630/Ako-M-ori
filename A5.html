Nau mai, haere mai ki Ako Māori. No matter where you are on your journey of language learning, we welcome you with open arms.

Our aim at Ako Māori is to provide you with a rich and immersive platform to learn, explore and nurture your knowledge of te reo Māori. We have curated a range of resources, tools and activities that will empower you to deepen your connection with this beautiful taonga.

As you navigate through the depths of te ao Māori, we hope that you will find our platform as not just a learning tool, but a whare of inspiration and cultural enrichment.

Together, let us contribute to the revitalisation of te reo Māori, an essential part of Aotearoa's heritage. Kia kaha, be brave, and embrace this exciting journey.
